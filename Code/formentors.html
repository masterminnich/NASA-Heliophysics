Instructions to post research opportunities

To post research opportunities, please visit the NASA's intern website: https://intern.nasa.gov/. Notice that previous intern website, OSSI (One Stop Shopping Initiative) application is retired as of May 31, 2018 and is no longer accessible to students or NASA users.

Important

Write "SESI" as the first word of your title, so it goes to the right program. Example: "SESI-'Your research opportunity title here'". All interns (including foreign nationals and lawful permanent residents use search features to find SESI opportunities).

Do not send your funds to Office of Education, they will have to be sent to CUA.

Write your name in your posted research opportunity, so we can identify you as a mentor.

Please, let somebody from the SESI team know that you submitted a research opportunity.

Send the information of the student/s you will be having (name, institution, title of the project, undergraduate or graduate status, etc.) to the SESI team as soon as the student accepts the internship.
